About Us
Centric Riders Sagar (CRS) was founded in 2020 when a few passionate individuals with immense fervour for riding came together to form a community of spirited bike enthusiasts.

Cherishing our motto ALL FOR ONE, ONE FOR ALL, the CRS family share a bond of trust and ardour which keeps strengthening over the many rides we organize.

The aim is to celebrate the art of riding prioritizing the proper practices of safety and caution just as much as adrenaline and adventure.

CRS also makes constant efforts to make a difference in the society by spreading awareness through the agenda of our rides; like escorting the 'Golden Victory Torch' from Sagar to Malthone, the Justice for Women Ride, donation drives, the Helmet Awareness Ride, the Plantation Ride, and Freedom Rides are a few of the many other initiatives taken by CRS.

A family of motorcyclists, where laughter is just as loud as the revving of our bike engines, where mutual trust and respect is fuelled by our shared passion for riding, where we are all for one, and one for all', CRS looks forward to carry the legacy of our biker brotherhood, share experiences and make many memories together along the miles that are yet to be motored.

Continuing our journey on this road of passion, we keep looking for opportunities to expand our community as well as enunciate our agenda of service and cognizance in the society.
